# **App Name**: GoldenHeir

## Core Features:

- Digital Gold Investment: Allow users to invest in digital gold for their children.
- Milestone Jewelry Conversion: Enable the conversion of digital gold into milestone jewelry.
- Digital Baby Journal: Provide a platform for creating and managing a digital baby journal with photos, videos, and text entries.
- Legacy Letters: Enable parents to write time-locked digital letters for their children.
- Gift Registry: Create a gift registry where friends and family can contribute to a child's gold fund.
- AI-Powered Jewelry Recommendations: Tool that recommends milestone jewelry designs based on the amount of gold invested and the child's age.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Rich gold (#B8860B) to convey wealth, security, and legacy.
- Background color: Light cream (#F5F5DC), subtly desaturated, for a soft, inviting feel.
- Accent color: Deep brown (#8B4513), analogous to the primary color, to highlight key actions.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a serif with an elegant, fashionable feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a sans-serif, to pair with 'Playfair', providing a little warmth and personality for longer text.
- Use refined, minimalist icons from Remixicon to maintain a clean, elegant design.
- Subtle animations and transitions to enhance user engagement.
